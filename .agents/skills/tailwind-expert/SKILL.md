## 🎨 Philosophy: Premium Aesthetics are Mandatory
A project is judged by its first impression. We don't build generic apps; we build premium digital experiences.

## ⚖️ Pareto Rule: 20% of effort for 80% of aesthetics
1. **Design Tokens**: Standardize colors, spacing, and shadows. Don't use `bg-red-500` or `m-4`. Use a curated palette.
2. **Glassmorphism & Gradients**: Use `backdrop-blur`, subtle borders, and gradients (`from-via-to`) to add depth.
3. **Typography Scaling**: Use `text-balance`, `text-pretty`, and a solid font hierarchy (heading -> subhead -> body).
4. **Spacing Harmony**: Stick to the 4px or 8px grid (1rem = 16px). Consistency in margins and padding is 80% of layout.

## 💅 Styling Directives:
- **HSL over HEX**: Easier to control light and saturation.
- **Subtle Micro-animations**: Use `transition`, `duration`, and `ease-in-out` on everything interactive.
- **Semantic HTML**: Use `<header>`, `<main>`, `<section>`, `<article>`. It's good for SEO and Accessibility.
- **Tailwind v4+**: Use the modern `@theme` or `@import "tailwindcss";` approach.
